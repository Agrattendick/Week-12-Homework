Conclusions

Our group looked into the effects Latitude had on given cities. We looked into the correlation between Latitude and Wind Speeds, cloudiness, Humidity and max temp. In retrospect it we should have accounted for more variables than just Latitude. Altitude is easy to measure, and might account for some of the variation in Max Temperature. Distance from a large body of water, even limiting things to Oceans and Seas would likely clean up cloudiness and Humidity. Wind Speed would also see some cleaner correlation from the above additions.
